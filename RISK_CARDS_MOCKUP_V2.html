Design Notes (Military/Industrial Style)

Color Scheme: Military/industrial colors matching rd-dashboard:

  • Critical Risk: #8B0000 (Dark military red)
  • At Risk: #B45F06 (Military amber)
  • On Track: #2D4F1E (Dark military green)
  • Completed: #4A5D23 (Olive green)

Key Features:

  • Clean, minimal design with shadcn/ui style
  • Status badges in card headers
  • Lucide React icons for visual indicators
  • Progress bars for risk mitigation tracking
  • Hover effects on cards
  • 3-column responsive grid (lg:grid-cols-3)
  • Muted, professional color palette
